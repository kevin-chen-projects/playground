Bare DAW

a tiny browser DAW

Instruments

Drag an instrument into the timeline to add a track.

Sections (song structure)

  1. + New adds another section (verse, chorus…).
  2. Duplicate copies the current section.
  3. Toggle Loop → Song to chain sections.

Drum tracks

  1. Click cells to toggle hits.

Piano roll

  1. Click a cell to place a note; drag right to lengthen.
  2. Click a note to remove it.
  3. Click a piano key to audition.

Typing notes

  1. Click a melodic track to select it.
  2. Whites: A S D F G H J K
  3. Blacks: W E T Y U
  4. [ ] octave · ← → cursor · 1–8 length · ⌫ undo

MIDI keyboard

  1. Click MIDI: off in the toolbar to connect.
  2. Notes within 150 ms stack as a chord.
